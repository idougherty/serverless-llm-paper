We need to talk about Serverless LLMs

=== Once upon a time (context) ===
The demand for agentic AI has seen much growth, fueling upstream demand to offload this infrastructural burden to datacenters.
Agentic AI is characterized by smaller models (7-32B parameters) that rely on tool calling instead of learned parameters to achieve functional accuracy.

=== Villain (problem) ===
The required resource footprint to service a production scale serverless workload is impractical using existing methodologies.

=== Hero (Solution) ===
The smaller models LLMs that power agentic tools are right sized for local CPU inference, enabling the colocation of LLM inferencing and agent logic.

=== Results/What does it enable (happily ever after) ===
De-emphasizing the inference latency enables practical CPU inference.
It is tractible to do LLM serving on cheap, power efficient hardware.
